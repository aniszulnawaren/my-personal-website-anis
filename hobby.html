Hobby 👻

I enjoy listening to music while doing any type of work. It's like increasing my motivation to do something. For example, I enjoy cleaning the house while listening to music because it makes me more enthusiastic and indirectly sincere in doing the work. My life is incomplete without songs. I also listen to different genres of music such as hiphop, jazz, edm, techno, and the genre that is popular nowadays known as "jedag jedug". If I've heard a song, I will sing along with it and follow the rhythm.

My second hobby is quite useful which is baking cakes and selling them. Whenever I have free time, I will collect orders from friends, acquaintances or residents around Alor Setar and Pokok Sena and will do cash on delivery. During the Movement Control Order (MCO), my cake sale got a lot of orders. I am grateful for making a lot of profit and quietly taking the outside wind which at that time the whole of Malaysia was asked to stay at home hehehe. I also used to sell my cakes at my school and this was really unexpected because most of my teachers also supported my small business and kept repeating orders. This small business still continues today.p>

The last one is the most difficult hobby to overcome, which is that I am a sleepy girl. I think this is also one of my hobbies because I love to sleep so much till sometimes my parents scold me for always sleeping.
