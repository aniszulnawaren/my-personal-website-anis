About Suksis

Suksis is stand for Kor-Sukarelawan Polis Siswa Siswi. Among the objectives of the establishment of Suksis is to produce students who have a high sense of identity and discipline. The second is to form students who are intellectual and charismatic in leadership and the third is to cultivate the spirit of friendship among students of multiple races and religions.

The training provided is such as police basic training (Inspector), SUKSIS Training has been systematically organized so as not to interfere with student learning, learning about the act and section, firearm handling training and some basic courses such as the PGA Course which is conducted in the forest for approximately 5 days.
