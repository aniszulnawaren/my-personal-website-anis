About Friends

I have 7 friends including me and we have been friends for about 9 years since high school. One of them is my best friend, my roommate, my classmate, and maybe my soulmate? hahaha, we often go everywhere together till most of the time people often call us twins.

Two of my friends used to work at KLIA Kuala Lumpur and they are twin brothers. The other three are waiting for the university's decision to pursue a bachelor's degree and the other one is with me most of the time.

Since graduating from high school, we rarely see each other but always remember. The only way to meet is only on Hari Raya Aidilfitri because each will try to find time to spend time together.
